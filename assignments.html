Assignment 2:
:: Reverse engineer a computational artwork in Processing :: Take one artwork viewed in class this semester (or one you've found on your own and approved by the professor) and recreate it unsing the Processing programming language. The artwork should replicate the visual elements of the work as well as the mathematical and logical operations. Your code for this assignment should be heavily commented, demonstrating your "metacognition" of the logical and mathematical processes at play, and where you are stuck. Your comments should also explicate how this computational replication could be developed further. 20% of grade.
Due before class December 01.
